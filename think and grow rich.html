Think and grow rich - Napoleon Hill

"Think And Grow Rich" is an inspirational book serving as a personal development and self-motivation guide to many. First published in 1937, this book has made more millionaires than any other such book.

Andrew Carnegie's thought was the inspiration behind the author Napoleon Hill's idea of analyzing the self-made billionaires and learning from their experiences.

The thought is a thing. "If I want it, I'll have it" is the magical formula of the self-made billionaires. Learning and pratical implementation of the same will make you stand in the queue of the richest people of this world.
